# 🪖 Community Armies Overview

Welcome to the **Community Armies** section of CivicEdge!  
This feature allows citizens to actively contribute to society by joining service-based groups and participating in community-driven initiatives.

Community Armies transform civic responsibility into real-world action — enabling citizens to support causes they care about and help their communities grow stronger.

---

## What Can You Do?

### 🤝 Join Service Armies
Become part of purpose-driven armies such as Environment Army, Blood Donation Army, Emergency Volunteer Army, and more — based on your interests and skills.

### 📅 Participate in Community Events
Register for clean-up drives, awareness programs, donation camps, and emergency response activities organized under your army.

### 🚨 Respond to Emergencies
Receive targeted notifications during urgent situations and contribute where your help is needed most.

### 🏅 Track Your Contributions
View your participation history, service hours, and completed activities under your profile.

### 📜 Earn Certificates
Receive verified certificates for your community service, available for download or sharing.

---

## How It Works

1. **Browse available armies** based on different service categories.
2. **Join an army** — instantly or through eligibility validation depending on the type.
3. **Get notified** about upcoming or emergency events.
4. **Register for events** based on your availability.
5. **Participate and contribute** to the community.
6. **Receive recognition** through certificates and contribution records.
