🎭 Character 1: Citizen (The Voice of the People)

    "The one who notices problems, shares ideas, and takes the first step."

    Role: The citizen is the initiator — without them, the system stays silent.

    Motivation: Wants to make the place they live in cleaner, safer, and smarter.

    Key Actions:

        Report complaints with images/maps

        Suggest ideas and improvements

        Join community armies

        Participate in forums and discussions

        Earn recognition for contribution

📌 Tagline: “I see, I speak, I change.”
🎭 Character 2: Solver (The Society in Action)

    "They are the hands that fix — the physical response to digital voices."

    Role: The solver is the societal response — either civic workers or organized community volunteers who resolve the reported issues.

    Motivation: Wants to serve the community and make a visible impact.

    Key Actions:

        Receive and act on assigned issues

        Upload progress and solutions

        Share success stories (pending approval)

        Respond to service requests

        Earn rewards based on contribution

📌 Tagline: “Your problem, my action.”
🎭 Character 3: Admin (The Bridge Between Voice and Action)

    "The one who ensures fairness, order, and flow between citizens and solvers."

    Role: The admin is the overseer and coordinator who manages users, assigns tasks, approves content, and monitors data.

    Motivation: Wants efficient governance and healthy civic participation.

    Key Actions:

        Assign solvers to issues

        Monitor and approve forum content

        Push notifications for alerts/emergencies

        Track performance and generate analytics

        Manage rewards and roles

📌 Tagline: “I turn your voices into impact.”
